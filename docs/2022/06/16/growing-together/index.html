Growing Together

Carlos David
2022-06-16
We grow together through spirited teamwork

Given the vast amount of exixting knowledge, it is very difficult to know everything, which is why we are generally very good at certain topics and not at others, so it is important to rely on people who know more than one topic, which can be difficult for us, and vice versa when we have greater ability in some subject we can support others.

During the DSU I have supported my classmates in the subjects that are difficult for them, just as they have supported me in the subjects that are difficult for me, for example, in the Google Cloud Platform fundamentals course, my project partner was Raul and we had to deploy our application using gcp services for that task I had already had some experience with the services to use, however we decided that Raul do the deployment since this way he could practice and learn more and I helped him when he got blocked . And for my part, the last time we were in the office, I taught the two of them about Github actions cuz I was taking a course on udemy and it think that they could become interested, so we had a small class on Github actions. and I also made a small example on docker volumes.

These are some examples of how we help each other and how we grow together in such a way that we improve our skills together to be better professionals.
