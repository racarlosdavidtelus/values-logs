Knowing The Business

Carlos David
2022-06-15
We embrace change and innovate courageously

At work, we are generally part of a single team, so one is usually limited to knowing what is important for the team. However, I consider that it is important to know as much as possible about the business and be prepared for a possible change of team or simply to know more about the business because this can help us to solve more general problems if we see the complete panorama and not only what affects us directly.

So for that reason I asked my other mentors who are in different projects than I, if they could meet with me in a 1 on 1 session so that they could talk to me about what they do in the company and in this way I can have a broader view of the company beacuse is important to be prepared for change cuz in the tech industry you have to be dynamic not static and be ready if there is some change.
