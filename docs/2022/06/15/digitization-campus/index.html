Digitization Campus

Carlos David
2022-06-15
We passionately put our customers and communities first

Working from home became popular due to the pandemic and the main advantage, as I see it is to avoid traffic cuz we can spend hours in the car if we don’t leave home early, however there is something that working from home cannot replace and that is interaction in real time with our team, there is no delay for bad internet connection and we can have a fluid and warner conversation with our peers and not through a screen, another advantage is that in these personal conversations they can arise ideas that give way to the continuous improvement of the projects in which the team members are working.

Since the beginning of the DSU I did not had the opportunity to interact with my peers and mentors in person, of course we had communication through video calls, but the opportunity presented to us to be all together in the office because the digitization campus was inaugurated and for that reason we went to the office to work and it was a very good experience because as mentioned before it is very different work from home being alone than work in the office with all the team and we have better human connections and these connections generate a synergy that derives in giving a better product to the client.
